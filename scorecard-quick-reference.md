# BDPL Scorecard Quick Reference Guide

> **Note:** This is a quick reference guide only. It does not replace the official BDPL League Regulations. In any dispute, the official regulations apply. For questions, contact your divisional representative via your divisional WhatsApp group.

---

## Match Format

The league uses a **4-2-4 format**:

| Phase | Frames | Type |
|-------|--------|------|
| First | S1, S2, S3, S4 | Singles |
| Middle | P1, P2 | Doubles (Pairs) |
| Last | S5, S6, S7, S8 | Singles |

---

## Player Requirements

| Players | Result |
|---------|--------|
| **6-12 players** | Full match, no forfeits |
| **5 players** | Start 2-0 down (forfeit P2 and S6) |
| **Fewer than 5** | Cannot fulfil fixture |

**Frame limits per player:** No player may participate in more than two frames per match. This means either two singles **OR** one singles and one doubles.

---

## Signing In

### Deadlines

| Time | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **20:15** | All players should be at venue |
| **20:20** | Home team signed in (minimum 5 players) |
| **20:25** | Away team signed in (minimum 5 players) |
| **20:30** | Match must start |

### Late Penalties

| Time | Penalty |
|------|---------|
| **Not ready by 20:45** | Forfeit S1 and S2 + £10 fine |
| **Not ready by 21:00** | Forfeit entire match (10-0) + £10 fine |

### How to Sign In

- Each player writes their name legibly in the Name column
- Each player signs in the Signature column next to their name
- Players must be signed in by the deadline to be eligible to play

---

## Filling In the Scorecard

### Recording Frame Results

- Mark an **X** in the Win column for the winning player/pair

### Optional Stat Tracking

If a player wants break and dish or reverse dish tracked, mark the Win column with:

| Mark | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **B** | Break and dish (broke and cleared the table) |
| **R** | Reverse dish (opponent broke, you cleared the table) |

### Player Positioning Rules (6-12 Players)

- **S1-S4:** Four different players play the first four singles
- **P1-P2:** Doubles pairs must be declared before the first doubles frame begins
- **S5-S8:** Four players play the last four singles
- **S7 and S8:** Must be played by two players from S1-S4 (their second singles frame)
- **Two singles rule:** Players who play two singles frames (S1-S4 and again in S7-S8) **cannot** also play doubles

### Five Players Only

If your team has only 5 players:

- You start **2-0 down**
- **P2** is forfeited (write "Forfeit" for both player names)
- **S6** is forfeited (write "Forfeit" for player name)
- Your 5 players fill S1-S4, P1, S5, S7, S8

---

## End of Match

### Captain Signatures

Both captains **must** sign the scorecard at the end of the match. By signing, captains confirm:

- The result is accurate
- All players were eligible
- They accept the final score

**Important:** Signing waives the right to dispute the match result later (except for conduct issues).

### Final Score

Write the total frames won by each team in the Final Score boxes at the bottom.

---

## Submitting the Result

| Deadline | Penalty |
|----------|---------|
| **15:00 next day** | No penalty |
| **After 15:00** | £5 fine |
| **After 20:00 Friday** | £10 fine + Committee may void/award match |

**How to submit:** Photo of completed scorecard to your Divisional WhatsApp group.

## Points

| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Win | 2 |
| Draw | 1 |
| Loss | 0 |

---

## Example Scorecard Explained

The example scorecard shows:

**Home Team (Team A)** - 6 players available:
- Players 1-4 play S1-S4 (first singles)
- Players 5 & 6 play P1
- Players 1 & 2 play P2 (one singles + one doubles each = 2 frames)
- Players 5 & 6 play S5 & S6 (their second frame, one singles + one doubles each)
- Players 3 & 4 play S7 & S8 (their second singles, so they couldn't play doubles)

**Away Team (Team B)** - Only 5 players available:
- Starts 2-0 down
- P2 and S6 marked as "Forfeit"
- 5 players fill remaining frames

**Result:** 5-5 draw (each team gets 1 point)
