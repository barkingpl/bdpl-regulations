# BDPL 2026 — Captain's Quick Start Guide

Everything you need to know to run your team. For full details, see the [Complete Regulations](regulations-full.md).

---

## MATCH NIGHT CHECKLIST

### Before You Arrive
- [ ] Confirm 5+ players available
- [ ] Know your line-up (S1-S8 and doubles pairings)
- [ ] Check you have transport to venue

### Key Deadlines

| Time | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| **20:30** | All players must be signed in (minimum 5) |
| **20:45** | Not ready? Forfeit S1 & S2 + £10 fine |
| **21:00** | Not ready? Forfeit entire match 10-0 + £10 fine |

> **WARNING:** It is the HOME team's responsibility to provide the official BDPL scorecard. No scorecard = 1 league point deducted regardless of result!

---

## MATCH FORMAT

**10 frames total:**
- S1, S2, S3, S4 (singles)
- D1, D2 (doubles)
- S5, S6, S7, S8 (singles)

### Player Rules
- Each player can play **maximum 2 singles**
- If playing 2 singles: must be once in S1-S4 and once in S7-S8 (NOT S5 or S6)
- Players doing 2 singles **cannot play doubles**
- Doubles players (P1 & P2) must only play 1 singles each

### Points
- Win = 2 points
- Draw = 1 point
- Loss = 0 points

---

## AFTER THE MATCH

### Result Submission
1. Take a clear photo of the completed scorecard
2. Post to your Divisional WhatsApp group
3. **Deadline: Tuesday 23:59**
4. Late submission = £10 fine

---

## PLAYER REGISTRATION

- All players must be registered **before** they play
- Unregistered player = £10 fine + frames awarded to opponents
- Players can only be registered with ONE team

### Competition Eligibility
Most competitions require **4 league matches** played during the season.

---

## MEETINGS

### Delegates Meetings (3 per season)

| Missed | Penalty |
|--------|---------|
| 1st | £25 fine |
| 2nd | £25 fine + 1 point deduction |
| 3rd | Committee discretion |

Penalties are **cumulative** — miss both = £50 + 1 point.

### AGM (December)
- Missing = £10 fine
- Any team member can attend

## POSTPONEMENTS

- Request via Committee **as early as possible**
- Granted at **Committee discretion only**
- Rearranged matches must be played within set timeframe
- Failure to rearrange = match may be awarded to other team

---

## GRIEVANCES

1. Raise in Divisional WhatsApp first
2. If unresolved → Division Support person reviews
3. If still unresolved → Full Committee decides
4. **Committee decision is final** — no appeals
