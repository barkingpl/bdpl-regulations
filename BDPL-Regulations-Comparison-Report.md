# BDPL Regulations Comparison Report

## Purpose
This document compares the **OLD** regulations (League_Regulations.pdf - 2024) against the **NEW** regulations (regulations-full.md - 2026) to ensure all content has been carried across.

---

## SUMMARY OF FINDINGS

### ✅ Items Successfully Carried Across
The vast majority of content has been transferred and often improved/expanded.

### ⚠️ Items Requiring Attention
The following items from the old regulations appear to be **MISSING** or **SIGNIFICANTLY CHANGED** in the new version:

| Item | Old Regulation | Status in New | Recommendation |
|------|----------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Practice Frame** | Home team must allow 1 frame practice for away team (5.1) | ❌ NOT FOUND | Add to Section 4.7 |
| **Stalemate Rule** | 5 shots to pot legal ball before re-rack (5.3) | ❌ NOT FOUND | Add to Section 2.4 |
| **Refreshments Compensation** | £10 compensation for refreshments if team no-shows (3.12) | ❌ NOT FOUND | Add to walkovers section |
| **Player Transfer Rule** | One player can transfer if attended 75%+ but NOT played singles (3.6) | ❌ NOT FOUND | Add to Section 3.2 or 3.3 |
| **Delegates Meeting Penalties** | Escalating: £10, then £10+2pts, then £10+5pts for consecutive misses (1.3) | ⚠️ CHANGED | New version has different structure |
| **Second Season Registration** | £80 fee for teams joining in 2nd season (2.6) | ❌ NOT FOUND | Add if still applicable |
| **Minimum 6 Players** | Old says minimum 6 players, new says minimum 5 | ⚠️ CHANGED | Intentional change - verify |
| **Joker Rule Reference** | Old 5.2 mentions joker rule only for league games | ❌ NOT FOUND | Add if still in use |
