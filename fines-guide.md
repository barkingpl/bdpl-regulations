# BDPL 2026 — Fines & Disciplinary Guide

Complete reference of all penalties. Use Ctrl+F / Cmd+F to search.

---

## FINANCIAL PENALTIES

### Player Eligibility

| Offence | Fine | What Else Happens |
|---------|------|-------------------|
| Playing **unregistered player** | **£10** per player | All frames involving that player awarded to opponents |
| Playing **suspended player** | **£50** | All frames awarded to opponents + further sanctions likely |
| Player **registered with two teams** | **£50** | Player suspended; frames count as losses; may be expelled |

---

### Match Start & Timing

| Offence | Fine | What Else Happens |
|---------|------|-------------------|
| Fewer than 5 players signed by 20:30 | **£10** | Frames forfeited |
| Not ready by **20:45** | **£10** | S1 & S2 forfeited (start 2-0 down) |
| Not ready by **21:00** | **£10** | Entire match awarded 10-0 |
| Line-up/signing breach | **£10** | Affected frames awarded to opponents |
| Doubles declaration breach | **£10** | Doubles frames awarded to opponents |

---

### Result Submission

| Offence | Fine |
|---------|------|
| Late result (after Tuesday 23:59) | **£10** |
| Using unofficial scorecard (away team) | Warning first, then **£10** |

---

### Meeting Attendance

| Meeting Missed | Fine | Point Deduction |
|----------------|------|-----------------|
| 1st delegates meeting | **£25** | None |
| 2nd delegates meeting | **£25** | **1 league point** |
| 3rd delegates meeting | Committee discretion | Committee discretion |
| AGM (December) | **£10** | None |

> **Note:** Delegates meeting penalties are **cumulative**. Missing meetings 1 AND 2 = £50 total + 1 point deducted.

---

## POINT DEDUCTIONS

| Offence | Deduction |
|---------|-----------|
| Home team cannot provide official scorecard | **1 league point** per occurrence |
| Missing 2nd delegates meeting | **1 league point** |

### Scorecard Point Deduction Examples

| Match Result | Normal Points | Without Official Scorecard |
|--------------|---------------|---------------------------|
| Win | 2 points | **1 point** |
| Draw | 1 point | **0 points** |
| Loss | 0 points | **-1 point** |

---

## FRAME FORFEITURES

| Situation | Frames Lost |
|-----------|-------------|
| Not ready by 20:45 | S1 & S2 |
| Not ready by 21:00 | All 10 frames (match 10-0) |
| Unregistered player played | All frames involving that player |
| Line-up breach (wrong positions) | Affected frames |
| Doubles pairing breach | Affected doubles frames |

---

## PAYMENT RULES

- All fines must be paid within **14 days**
- Outstanding fines may result in:
  - Additional points deductions
  - Suspension from competitions
  - Team suspension from league

---

## SERIOUS MISCONDUCT

The Committee may impose additional sanctions for serious incidents:

| Sanction | For What |
|----------|----------|
| Match ban | Misconduct, abuse, unsporting behaviour |
| Extended ban | Repeated offences, serious incidents |
| Season ban | Violence, threats, bringing league into disrepute |
| Lifetime ban | Extreme cases |
| Expulsion | Teams or individuals at Committee discretion |

---

## CONDUCT REMINDER

> "All members of the League must act at all times in a friendly, sporting, honest and helpful way."

Exploiting loopholes or acting contrary to the spirit of the rules may result in sanctions even if no specific rule is broken.

---

## DISPUTES & APPEALS

- **Committee decisions are final**
- **There is no appeals process**
- Grievances should be raised in Divisional WhatsApp first

---

## FINE PAYMENT

Contact the Committee via official channels to arrange payment.
